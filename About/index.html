About Me

With over seven years of experience in the design industry, I have honed my skills as both a Web Designer and Senior Analyst at Personiv Manila. My journey began as a Web Designer, where I developed a keen eye for aesthetics and user experience. I later transitioned to a Senior Analyst role, allowing me to deepen my understanding of data-driven design and project management.

I specialize in website development using platforms like WordPress and Duda, combining creativity with technical expertise to deliver visually appealing and functional websites. My freelance experience has further enriched my skill set, enabling me to work on diverse projects and collaborate with various clients.

I am passionate about creating engaging digital experiences that not only meet client expectations but also resonate with users. Let's connect and bring your vision to life!
